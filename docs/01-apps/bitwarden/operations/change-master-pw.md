# Change master password

Change your Bitwarden master password.

## Steps

1. Open the Help Center.

    [https://bitwarden.com/help/](https://bitwarden.com/help/)

2. Type `how to change master password` in the **Search** box.

3. Open the **Change master password** section.

       [https://bitwarden.com/help/master-password/#change-master-password](https://bitwarden.com/help/master-password/#change-master-password)

## Note

You must know your current master password to set a new one.
