# Disable Google Photos backup on Pixel 8a

Disable Google Photos backup to prevent photos and videos from uploading to your Google Account. This setting is controlled inside the Google Photos application.

## Steps

1. Open **Google Photos**.

2. Tap your **profile picture** in the top-right corner.

3. Tap **Photos settings**.

4. Select **Backup**.

5. Turn **Backup** **Off**.

## Note

* Disabling backup prevents **new photos and videos** from uploading to your Google Account.
* Photos already backed up to Google Photos remain stored in the cloud.
* To remove existing backups, delete them from **[https://photos.google.com](https://photos.google.com)**.
