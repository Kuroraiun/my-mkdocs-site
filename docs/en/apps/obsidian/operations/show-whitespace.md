# Show whitespace

Display whitespace characters in Obsidian using the **Show Whitespace** plugin.

## Steps

1. Open **Settings**.

    ![Screenshot](show-whitespace-01.webp)

2. Click **Browse**.

    ![Screenshot](show-whitespace-02.webp)

3. Search for **Show Whitespace**.

    ![Screenshot](show-whitespace-03.webp)

4. Click **Install**.

    ![Screenshot](show-whitespace-04.webp)

5. Click **Enable**.

    ![Screenshot](show-whitespace-05.webp)
