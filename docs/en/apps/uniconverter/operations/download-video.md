# Download a YouTube video

1. Click **Start Now**.
   ![Screenshot](download-video-01.png)

2. Log in to YouTube.
   ![Screenshot](download-video-02.png)

3. **Paste** the video URL, then click **Download**.
   ![Screenshot](download-video-03.png)

4. To download another video, click **Downloading** to return to the previous screen.
   ![Screenshot](download-video-04.png)
