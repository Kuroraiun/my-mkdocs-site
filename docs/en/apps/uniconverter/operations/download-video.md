# Download a YouTube video

1. Click **Start Now**.
   ![Screenshot](download-video-01.webp)

2. Log in to YouTube.
   ![Screenshot](download-video-02.webp)

3. **Paste** the video URL, then click **Download**.
   ![Screenshot](download-video-03.webp)

4. To download another video, click **Downloading** to return to the previous screen.
   ![Screenshot](download-video-04.webp)
