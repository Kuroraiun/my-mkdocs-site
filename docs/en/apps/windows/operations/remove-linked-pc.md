# Remove a PC from the Microsoft account
1. Click **Linked devices**.
![Screenshot 01](remove-linked-pc-01.webp)
1. Click **Remove device**. 
![Screenshot 02](remove-linked-pc-02.webp)
1. Click **Remove this device**.
![Screenshot 03](remove-linked-pc-03.webp)
1. Select **I'm ready to remove this device**, then click **Remove**.
![Screenshot 04](remove-linked-pc-04.webp)
1. Click **Remove from Microsoft Store**.
![Screenshot 05](remove-linked-pc-05.webp)
1. Click **Unlink**.
![Screenshot 06](remove-linked-pc-06.webp)
1. Click **Unlink**.
![Screenshot 07](remove-linked-pc-07.webp)
1. Click **Close**.
![Screenshot 07](remove-linked-pc-08.webp)
