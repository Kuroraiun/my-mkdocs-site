# Convert CRW to JPG

1. Read the message.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-01.webp) 

1. Select **Install**.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-02.webp) 

1. Click the download link.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-03.webp) 

1. Download the installer.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-04.webp)

1. Click **More info**.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-05.webp)

1. Click **Next**.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-06.webp)

1. Click **Next**.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-07.webp)

1. Click **Next**.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-08.webp)

1. Click **Finish**.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-09.webp)

1. Open the **CRW** file.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-10.webp)

1. Select **Export**.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-11.webp)

1. Adjust the settings, then export.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-12.webp)

1. The conversion is complete.

    ![Screenshot](convert-crw-to-png-13.webp)
