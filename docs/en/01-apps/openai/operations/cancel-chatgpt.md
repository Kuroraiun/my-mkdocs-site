# Cancel a ChatGPT subscription

1. Select **Settings**.
    ![Screenshot](cancel-chatgpt-01.png)

1. Select **Account**.
    ![Screenshot](cancel-chatgpt-02.png)

1. Select **Manage** > **Cancel Subscription**.
    ![Screenshot](cancel-chatgpt-03.png)

1. Click **Cancel subscription**.
    ![Screenshot](cancel-chatgpt-04.png)

1. Confirm the expiration date.
    ![Screenshot](cancel-chatgpt-05.png)
