# Remove a PC from the Microsoft account
1. Click **Linked devices**.
![Screenshot 01](images/remove-pc-from-account01.png)
1. Click **Remove device**. 
![Screenshot 02](images/remove-pc-from-account02.png)
1. Click **Remove this device**.
![Screenshot 03](images/remove-pc-from-account03.png)
1. Select **I'm ready to remove this device**, then click **Remove**.
![Screenshot 04](images/remove-pc-from-account04.png)
1. Click **Remove from Microsoft Store**.
![Screenshot 05](images/remove-pc-from-account05.png)
1. Click **Unlink**.
![Screenshot 06](images/remove-pc-from-account06.png)
1. Click **Unlink**.
![Screenshot 07](images/remove-pc-from-account07.png)
1. Click **Close**.
![Screenshot 07](images/remove-pc-from-account08.png)
