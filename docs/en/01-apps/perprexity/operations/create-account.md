# Create a Perplexity account

1. Close the window.
   ![Screenshot](../images/create-account01.png)

2. Click **Sign In**.
   ![Screenshot](../images/create-account02.png)

3. Enter your email address.
   ![Screenshot](../images/create-account03.png)

4. Confirm your email address.
   ![Screenshot](../images/create-account04.png)

5. Enter the verification code.
   ![Screenshot](../images/create-account05.png)

6. The account is created.
   ![Screenshot](../images/create-account06.png)
