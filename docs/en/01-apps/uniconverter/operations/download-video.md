# Download a YouTube video

1. Click **Start Now**.
   ![Screenshot](../00-images/download-video01.png)

2. Log in to YouTube.
   ![Screenshot](../00-images/download-video02.png)

3. **Paste** the video URL, then click **Download**.
   ![Screenshot](../00-images/download-video03.png)

4. To download another video, click **Downloading** to return to the previous screen.
   ![Screenshot](../00-images/download-video04.png)
